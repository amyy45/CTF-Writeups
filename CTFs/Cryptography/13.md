## üß† 2. Initial Thoughts / Approach
Given the *Cryptography* category and *Easy* difficulty, I expected:

- A classical substitution cipher  
- No file interaction or complex tooling  
- A direct transformation of the given text  

Since the challenge name is **13** and the description explicitly mentions **ROT13**, the encoding method was immediately clear.

ROT13 is a Caesar cipher that shifts each letter **13 positions** in the alphabet while leaving numbers and symbols unchanged.
